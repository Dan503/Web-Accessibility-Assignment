Welcome to Plumtree

Where children flourish

Plumtree is a not-for-profit organisation that provides support for young children aged birth to 8 years old with a developmental delay or disability and their families.

Operating for over 30 years, our vision is for a society where children with disabilities and developmental delays, and their families, are supported to have a full life in the community.

Our purpose is to empower families to make this happen.
